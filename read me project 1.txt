# Analysis of Official Development Assistance (ODA) from DAC Donors

## Overview
This project analyzes trends in Official Development Assistance (ODA) from Development Assistance Committee (DAC) donor countries between 2005 and 2024.

## Objective
- Analyze donor contributions
- Examine donor ranking stability
- Identify concentration of development assistance among major donors
- Explore long-term aid trends

## Tools
Power BI  
Excel  
DAX

## Key Insights
- A small number of countries dominate global development assistance.
- The United States remains the largest DAC donor.
- Germany and the United Kingdom are consistently among the top contributors.
- Overall ODA flows increased significantly after 2015.
